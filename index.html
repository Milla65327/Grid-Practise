Don't sweat the spills

Our olefin fabric is virtually stain-proof thanks to a deep dyeing process that colors each individual strand to the core, as opposed to other fibers that have little pores that absorb stains. You can even spray spills with a water and bleach solution (yes, we said bleach) and blot it out to clean stains.
